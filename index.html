About Cressida AgroTrade

Connecting Nigerian Producers
to Global Markets

Our company focuses on the aggregation, sourcing, and export facilitation of premium Nigerian agricultural commodities, connecting producers with international markets through structured supply chains, quality assurance, and efficient logistics coordination.

Every commodity that passes through our network is aggregated from trusted grower communities, inspected against export specifications, and prepared for dispatch with full documentation — giving international buyers a dependable partner on the ground in Nigeria.

Operations

Built on Structure,
Backed by Quality

From farmgate to warehouse, every step is documented, inspected, and prepared to export specification.

  1. 01

    Sourcing & Aggregation

    Our field teams work directly with grower networks across Nigeria’s producing regions, aggregating raw commodities at the farmgate level.

  2. 02

    Quality Control & Inspection

    Every lot is sampled, graded, and inspected against export-quality specifications before acceptance into our warehouses.

  3. 03

    Warehousing & Storage

    Commodities are stored in structured, moisture-controlled facilities that preserve quality from intake to dispatch.

  4. 04

    Export Preparation

    Cleaned, bagged, and documented to buyer specification — ready for inland haulage and port handling.

Logistics & Export

From Inland Aggregation
to International Port

  1. 01

  2. 02

    Port Documentation & Customs

  3. 03
